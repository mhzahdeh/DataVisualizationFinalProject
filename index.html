About the Project

This project explores global economic trends to identify the best countries for young individuals to start their professional careers. It combines my passion in global macroeconomics with data visualization to tell a story.

Project Objectives

  • Showcase which countries offer the best starting points for young professionals globally.
  • Highlight where economic success can be achieved over the long term.
  • Visualize global wealth investments via hedge funds and high-net-worth individuals.
  • Identify the healthiest and happiest places to live based on life expectancy data.
